About me

I'm currently a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Biostatistics, Bloomberg School of Public Health, Johns Hopkins University, where I am advised by Professor Hongkai Ji and Professor Nilanjan Chatterjee.
Prior to Johns Hopkins University, I obtained a B.S. in mathematics from the Department of Mathematical Science, Tsinghua University in 2019. My bachelor thesis advisor is Professor Stephen Shing-Toung Yau.
